About

TalentSpark is a free program for CS students to help them land internships and jobs at top tech companies. It's a hands-on, 8-week program starting in Fall 2015 taught by experienced software developers in the industry.

In this 8-week program, we'll help accelerate your learning and spark your talent so you can land your dream internship and job.

Develop

Learn the highly-demanded skills necessary to build a polished app from start to finish.

Stand out

We'll help sharpen your interview skills and perfect your resume so you stand out from the crowd.

Network

Upon successful completion of this program, we'll connect you with startups and top tech companies.
